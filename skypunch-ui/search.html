Found him. John McCarthy—pioneer of AI.

Born:
September 4, 1927
Boston, Massachusetts, United States
Died:
October 24, 2011 (86)
Stanford, California, United States
Education:
Princeton University, California Institute of Technology
Awards:
Turing Award, IJCAI Award for Research Excellence
  • Invented Lisp, a programming language still used in symbolic AI
  • Pushed time-sharing systems, making modern OS and cloud computing possible
  • Championed the idea of machines that reason, learn, and understand language
  • Won the Turing Award (1971), AI's top honor

He taught at Stanford, believed in logic-based AI, and helped shape everything we now call "thinking machines."
